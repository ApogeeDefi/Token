APOGEE STRENGTHS

$APGE is a deflationary token that also redistributes coins to holders. There were only 10 million $APGE tokens minted with zero being minted in the future. 5% of each transaction is split into 2.5% which is burned and 2.5% which is deposited to lucky holders. This gives investors a strong reason to hodl. With each transaction, the amount of $APGE shrinks and the amount of tokens in each wallet grows.

NFT LOTTERIES

In the near future we will be comissioning and minting one-of-a-kind Apogee NFTs. These NFTs will be given away to $APGE holders in a lottery format using a smart contract or other program. These NFTs may become very valuable if the project gains value and has success. You don't want to miss out on the opportunity of winning an NFT that could be worth 1k..5k..50k..1M...who knows!
